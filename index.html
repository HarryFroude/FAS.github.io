About Us

Welcome to Fish Around, a family-run aquatic centre located in the heart of Hailsham on the High Street. Since we opened in May 2010, we have grown significantly and become a trusted local destination for aquatic supplies.

We specialise in freshwater tropical fish, goldfish, and a diverse selection of aquarium plants. We offer special orders, aquarium servicing, and expert advice to help you create and maintain beautiful aquatic environments.

Our friendly and knowledgeable team is dedicated to providing excellent service and fostering a vibrant aquatic community.
